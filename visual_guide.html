08 — Voice & Tone

How Trinket Speaks

Trinket's voice is the kind, unhurried friend who knows how to hold a moment carefully. Never corporate, never rushed.

✦ Do

"A quiet place for everything you want to keep."

Warm, poetic, personal. Invites rather than instructs.

✗ Don't

"Store your files in our secure cloud storage solution."

Cold and transactional. Reduces memories to data.

✦ Do

"You've added something beautiful to your archive."

Celebrates the act; acknowledges what was just saved.

✗ Don't

"Upload successful. Item saved to database."

Too system-like. No warmth, no recognition of the person.
